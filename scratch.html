Favorite Recipe

  1. Place Bare Chicken Nuggets in an air fryer.
    • use the reheat button at 300 degrees
    • reheat for 15 minutes
  2. Add your favorite dipping sauce.
    • I usually use polynesian from Chick-fil-A
    • another good option is mayo and ketchup mixed to make fry sauce
  3. Add a fresh fruit or vegetable for a side.
    • options include
      • oranges
      • peppers
      • celery
      • pickles
      • kiwi
      • carrots
